CrystalCollector Game


Rules

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals shown below. Clicking one will add its point total to your score.

You win if your total score matches the shown randomly-generated number, but you lose if you go over it.

The point value of each crystal is also randomly generated each game, and is hidden from you until you click on it.
